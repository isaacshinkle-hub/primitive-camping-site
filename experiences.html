Primitive Camping Experiences

Primitive Camping LLC offers small-group Southern Utah experiences built around primitive clothing, simple tools, fire skills, hiking, campfires, sleeping under the stars, and overnight camp routines.

Primitive Day Hike Adventure

Have fun on a 4 hour Primitive Adventure day hike while dressed in primitive clothing. Hikes are in the Zion National Park area but not inside the park itself. We go places known to have had native inhabitants and may include viewing petroglyphs. Come with your own primitive gear or we will provide ours — a no-hassle way to see some of the lesser-known attractive areas surrounding Zion National Park. Come adventure like a caveman!

Primitive Overnight Camping Adventure

Hike a few miles in primitive clothing, set up a primitive campsite, and sleep under the stars around a primitive fire (if local fire restrictions allow). Camp like a caveman in a shallow cave or under the stars, wrapped in an animal-skin blanket, surrounded by breathtaking scenery. Our most popular adventure — it’s what we specialize in.
