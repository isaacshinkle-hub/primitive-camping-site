About Primitive Camping LLC

Primitive Camping LLC was founded by Isaac Shinkle in Southern Utah as a place for like-minded adults to learn, practice, and enjoy primitive camping.

The theme is hands-on and old-school: leather gear, simple tools, fire skills, sandals, water bags, clay pots, campfires, sleeping under the stars, and memorable group adventures.

The goal is value to people over profit: build community, teach useful skills, and create fun outdoor experiences with clear expectations.
