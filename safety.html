Safety and Forms

Primitive camping should feel adventurous, not careless. PCA-SU trips are planned around participant readiness, weather, terrain, gear, and clear expectations before anyone heads out.

Required forms

All participants must complete and sign the required forms before attending any Primitive Camping LLC activity.

  • Liability waiver and release of liability
  • Participant information and emergency contact form
  • Gear rental agreement when rental items are used

Gear expectations by level

  • Beginner: gaiters over modern footwear are acceptable; undergarments permitted; basic water container required; sun protection recommended.
  • Intermediate: primitive sandals required; no modern undergarments; leather gear recommended; water bag and basic tools required.
  • Expert: fuller primitive immersion with bow drill and obsidian knife skills; no modern shelter; self-sufficiency expected.

Before each event

  • Activity details, meeting location, timing, and required gear will be shared with confirmed participants.
  • Participants should be honest about medical concerns, outdoor experience, footwear, hydration, and comfort level.
  • Weather, terrain, and safety conditions may change the plan or cancel an outing.

Important: This page is informational and not a substitute for final event instructions or legal waiver language. Safety decisions stay conservative and event-specific.
